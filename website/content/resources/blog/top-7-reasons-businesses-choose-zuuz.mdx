ZUUZ agents handle end-to-end tasks: reading signals from your tools, applying your policies, taking action across systems, and logging every step. They don't suggest what to do. They do it — with human oversight where it matters.

Most automation platforms force you to rebuild processes from scratch. ZUUZ imports your existing workflows from CRM, ERP, and ticketing systems, maps every step and decision point, and runs them with agents and human approvals. No rip-and-replace.

## 3. Permission-aware search with evidence

One search across email, docs, chats, tickets, and business records. Every answer cites its sources. Every result respects the permissions of the person asking. No data leaks. No shadow access.

Before any approval or action, ZUUZ assembles a Context Pack — the policy evidence, budget data, risk flags, prior history, and compliance docs an approver needs. Decisions happen in minutes instead of days because the evidence is already there.

ZUUZ is designed for fast deployment. Connect your tools, map your workflows, deploy agents — and start measuring actual time savings within the first two weeks. No six-month implementation cycles.

## 7. One platform, not ten point solutions

Instead of buying separate tools for email automation, document processing, meeting follow-ups, approval routing, and enterprise search — ZUUZ provides all of these as a unified platform with consistent governance and a single audit trail.
